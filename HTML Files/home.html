Welcome To Hotel Le Meridien

Le Meridien is an upscale, design-focused international hotel brand with a European perspective, formerly headquartered in France and in the United Kingdom.The brand was acquired by US-based Starwood Hotels & Resorts in 2005. Following the acquisition of Starwood by Marriott in 2015, it is now owned by Marriott International and has a portfolio of over 110 hotels.
